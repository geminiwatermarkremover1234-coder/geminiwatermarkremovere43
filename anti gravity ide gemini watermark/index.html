Gemini Video Logo Remover Questions

What video dimensions does this Gemini Video Watermark Remover support?

This tool supports exact 1280 x 720, 720 x 1280, 1920 x 1080, and 1080 x 1920 videos. Note that 1080p support is experimental, and there might be minor watermark removal issues on some clips.

Does the Gemini Logo Remover from Video upload my file?

No. The video is processed locally in your browser. The page does not send your selected video to a server.

What format does the cleaned video use?

The cleaned download uses H.264 encoded MP4. A recent browser with local WebCodecs and MP4 recording support is required.

Does this remove invisible watermarking or provenance data?

No. This tool targets the visible corner logo only. It does not remove invisible watermarking, metadata, or provenance signals.

Can I use this Gemini Video Watermark Remover online for free?

Yes. The supported single-video workflow runs in your browser for free. Batch processing and simultaneous queue conversions are available with the Premium upgrade.
